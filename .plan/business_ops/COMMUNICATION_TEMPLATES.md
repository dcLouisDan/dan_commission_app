# Communication Templates

Copy-paste these scripts to maintain professionalism and save time. Replace `[Bracketed Text]` with specific details.

## 1. Commission Acceptance & Invoice
**Subject**: Commission Accepted: [Character Name/Project Title] - [Your Name]

> Hi [Client Name],
>
> Thank you so much for commissioning me! I’ve reviewed your request and I’m happy to accept this project.
>
> **Project Summary**:
> - Type: [e.g., Full Body, Cel Shaded]
> - Character: [Character Name]
> - Price: $[Amount] USD
>
> I have sent an invoice to your PayPal email ([Email]). Please note that my workflow starts once the [100% / 50%] payment is received.
>
> You can review the Terms of Service here: [Link to TOS]
>
> Looking forward to working with you!
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

## 2. Sketch for Review
**Subject**: Update: Sketch for [Character Name]

> Hi [Client Name],
>
> Work has begun! Please see the attached sketch for your commission.
>
> **Action Required**:
> Please let me know if the pose, expression, and composition look correct.
> - If you need major changes (pose/outfit), please let me know NOW.
> - Once approved, I will proceed to lining and coloring, where only minor adjustments can be made.
>
> Let me know what you think!
>
> Thanks,
> [Your Name]

## 3. Delays (Proactive)
**Subject**: Update on your commission status

> Hi [Client Name],
>
> I’m writing to give you a quick update. Due to [reason: personal emergency / health / unexpected workload], I need to push back the delivery date by [Number] days.
>
> The new expected delivery date is [Date]. I apologize for this delay and appreciate your patience. I want to make sure I don't rush your piece so it gets the quality it deserves.
>
> Thank you,
> [Your Name]
